About BTC Price Predictor

Our Mission

BTC Price Predictor is a sophisticated tool that uses machine learning algorithms to predict Bitcoin price movements. Our ensemble model combines multiple prediction techniques to provide accurate forecasts.

How It Works

We use a combination of:

  • Random Forest Regression
  • Gradient Boosting
  • Support Vector Regression
  • Lasso Regression
These models analyze various technical indicators and market data to generate predictions.
